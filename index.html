Design principles

  1. Knowing the workflow is important.
  2. Knowing the status of a case is important.
  3. Indication of current progress status.
  4. Design simple yet informative.
  5. Adhere to a consistent design pattern
  6. Tone of voice, typography is important
  7. Know your audience. -- Browser support, accessibility compliances

Development principles

  1. Use of agile practice.
  2. Create epics, stories, tasks and sub tasks.
  3. Prioritise the tasks with the product owener.
  4. Define what is "done". (develop, test, deploy or otherwise).
  5. Conduct morning meetings (SCRUM meetings in Agile).
  6. Groom backlog and re-prioritise based on product owner needs.
  7. Continues delivery - Keeps product ownere happy.
